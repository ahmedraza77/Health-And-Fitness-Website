10 WEEK MASS BUILDING PROGRAM

Main Goal: Build Muscle
Program Duration: 10 Weeks
Days Per Week: 4 Days
Time Per Workout: 50 Mins
Equipment: Barbell, Bodyweight, Dumbbells, Machines


Monday (chest & triceps)

- Chest
barbell bench press (4 Sets, 8-10 Reps)
incline bench press (3 Sets, 8-6 Reps)
decline bench press (3 Sets, 8-6 Reps)
dumbbell flys (2 Sets, 8 Reps)
dumbbell pullover (2 Sets, 8 Reps)

- Triceps
tricep extension (4 Sets, 8-10 Reps)
tricep dip (3 Sets, 10 Reps)
tricep bench dip (3 Sets, 8 Reps)


Tuesday(back & biceps)

- Back
deadlift (5 Sets, 8-10 Reps)
chin up (2 Sets, 8 Reps)
one arm dumbbell row (3 Sets, 8 Reps)
seated row (2 Sets, 8 Reps)
close grip lat pull down (3 Sets, 8-10 Reps)

- biceps
standing barbell curl (3 Sets, 8-6 Reps)
close grip preacher curl (3 Sets, 8-6 Reps)
incline dumbbell curl (2 Sets, 12-14 Reps)
concentration curl (2 Sets, 10 Reps)


Thursday(shoulders & forearms)

- Shoulders
machine shoulder press (3 Sets, 10 Reps)
dumbbell reverse fly (3 Sets, 8-10 Reps)
military press (4 Sets, 10 Reps)
dumbbell lateral raise (2 Sets, 10 Reps)
dumbbell shrugs (2 Sets, 10 Reps)
upright row (2 Sets, 10 Reps)

- Forearms
standing wrist curl (4 Sets, 10 Reps)
barbell wrist curl (4 Sets, 10 Reps)


Friday(legs)

- Legs
squat (5 Sets, 10 Reps)
leg extension (3 Sets, 12 Reps)
leg curl (3 Sets, 12 Reps)

- Calves
standing calf raise (4 Sets, 12 Reps)
seated calf raise (2 Sets, 12 Reps)




DARK KNIGHT WORKOUT PROGRAM

Main Goal: Building Lean Muscle/Getting Ripped
Program Duration: 8 Weeks
Days Per Week: 5 Days
Time Per Workout: 50 Mins
Equipment: Barbell, Bodyweight, Dumbbells, Machines


Monday (chest & triceps)

- Chest
barbell bench press (4 Sets, 8-10 Reps)
incline dumbell flys (2 Sets, 8-6 Reps)
pushups (2 Sets, 10-15 Reps)
cable crossover (2 Drop Sets)
Smith Machine Close Grip Bench Press (4 Sets, 8 Reps)

- Triceps
Bench Dips (3 Sets, 8-10 Reps)
Cable Rope Overhead Triceps Extension (2 Sets, 10 Reps)
Triceps Pushdown - Rope Attachment (2 Sets, 8 Reps)
Lying Triceps Press (1 drop set)


Tuesday(biceps)

- biceps
standing barbell curl (4 Sets, 8-6 Reps)
concentration curls (3 Sets, 8-6 Reps)
hammer curls (3 Sets, 8-6 Reps)
standing bicep cable curls (3 Sets, 8-6 Reps)
Prechair (3 Sets, 8-6 Reps)
incline dumbbell curl (2 Sets, 12-14 Reps)


Wednesday(Quads/Hamstrings)

- Legs
Barbell Squat (4 Sets, 8-10 Reps)
Dumbell Lunges (3 Sets, 8 Reps)
Leg Extension (2 Sets, 8 Reps)
Dumbell Squat (2 Sets, 8 Reps)
Stiff-Legged Barbell Deadlift (4 Sets, 8-6 Reps)
Hyperextension (2 Sets, 8-6 Reps)
Lying Leg Curl (3 Sets, 8-10 Reps)


Thursday(Lats/Midback)

- Back
Wide Grip Lat Pulldown (4 Sets, 8-10 Reps)
V-bar Pulldown (3 Sets, 8 Reps)
one arm dumbbell row (3 Sets, 8 Reps)
T-bar row (3 Sets, 8 Reps)
seated cable row (3 Sets, 8 Reps)
close grip lat pull down (3 Sets, 8-10 Reps)

Friday(shoulders & forearms)

- Shoulders
machine shoulder press (3 Sets, 10 Reps)
dumbbell reverse fly (3 Sets, 8-10 Reps)
military press (4 Sets, 10 Reps)
dumbbell lateral raise (2 Sets, 10 Reps)
dumbbell shrugs (2 Sets, 10 Reps)
upright row (2 Sets, 10 Reps)

- Forearms
standing wrist curl (4 Sets, 10 Reps)
barbell wrist curl (4 Sets, 10 Reps)
